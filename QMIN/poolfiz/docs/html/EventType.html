enum EventType

Indicates the type of event.

Documentation

Indicates the type of event. Can be: NO_EVENT (nothing happened), STATE_CHANGE (a ball transitioned to a different motion state), BALL_COLLISION (two balls collided), RAIL_COLLISION (a ball collided with a rail), POCKETED (a ball was pocketed), CUE_STRIKE (the cue ball was struck by the cue successfully), MISCUE (the cue ball was struck by the cue unsuccessfully), UNKNOWN_EVENT (an unknown event).
