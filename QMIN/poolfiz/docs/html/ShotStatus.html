enum ShotStatus

Indicates the outcome of the shot.

Documentation

Indicates the outcome of the shot. Can be: OK (normal shot), MISSED_BALL (provided shot parameters resulted in cue missing cue ball), INVALID_CUE_PARAMS (provided shot parameters are out of range or not physically possible), POOLFIZ_ERROR (an error condition resulted within poolfiz), UNKNOWN_ERROR (an unknown error occurred).
